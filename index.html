    Grand Theft Auto V Review

    The remastered San Andreas gives a new look to its cars while at the same adding some new models. Here are our top 5 cars that will give you candy to the eyes.

    GTA 5: Top 5 cars you should look for

    The Super GT made its debut in San Andreas. There are two versions of it. One is a hard top and one is a targa top. The Super GT draws inspiration from a number of different vehicles such as the 1993 Mitsubishi 3000GT, the Nissan 300 ZX Z32 and the 1993 Toyota Supra.

    Super GT

    To find a Super GT in GTA: San Andreas, you will have to be patient. Compared to other cars, it spawns rarely in the game. Usually you should check out the Strip in Las Venturas. There is a high chance that the car will appear at night.

    Cheetah

    Inspired by the Ferrari Testarossa and especially the F512 TR model, this car not only provides high speed but only gives a slick and cool appearance just like its name. You can easily get this car in a lot of places in GTA: San Andreas. Their spawn locations are usually Rode, Paradiso, Old Venturas and Market. You can even import one at the import/export dock in San Fierro. However it would cost them around $84,000.
